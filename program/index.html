Program

Schedule of IWSECO-WEA 2014 

Monday, August 25, 2014

  • 09:00 – 09:30:   Opening
  • 09:30 – 10:30:    Keynote
    • Philippe Grosjean,  Numerical Ecology of aquatic systems laboratory, COMPLEXYS Research Institute, University of Mons, Belgium
      Do software evolve too fast? A biologist’s point of view (see details)
  • 10:30 – 11:00:    Coffee Break
  • 11:00 – 12:30:    Business and Social Track (15 min presentation per paper, followed by 30 min group discussion)
    • Evert Eckhardt, Erwin Kaats, Slinger Jansen and Carina Alves
      The Merits of a Meritocracy in Open Source Software Ecosystems
    • Konstantinos Manikas, Klaus Marius Hansen and Morten Kyng
      Governance Mechanisms for Healthcare Apps
    • Mircea Lungu, Nicole Haenni, Oscar Nierstrasz and Niko Schwarz
      A Quantitative Analysis of Developer Information Needs in Software Ecosystems
    • J Yates Monteith, John Mcgregor and John Ingram
      Scientific Research Software Ecosystems
  • 12:30 – 13:30:    Lunch Break
  • 13:30 – 15:00:    Architecture and Technology Track (15 min presentation per paper, followed by 30 min group discussion)
    • Floor Aarnoutse, Cassandra Renes, Remco Snijders and Slinger Jansen
      The Reality of an Associate Model – Comparing Partner Activity in the Eclipse Ecosystem
    • Boris Spasojevic, Mircea Lungu and Oscar Nierstrasz
      Towards faster method search through static ecosystem analysis
    • Jens Knodel, Matthias Naab and Dominik Rost
      Supporting Architects in Mastering the Complexity of Open Software Ecosystems
    • Simone Da Silva Amorim, John Mcgregor, Eduardo Almeida and Christina von Flach G. Chavez
      Flexibility in Ecosystem Architectures
  • 15:00 – 15:30:    Coffee Break
  • 15:30 – 16:30:    Open Discussion and Breakout Groups
  • 16:30 – 17:00:    Wrapup and Closing
