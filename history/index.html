History

WEA 2015

  • 3rd International Workshop on Ecosystem Architectures (WEA 2015)
  • Co-organized with the 7th Workshop on Software Ecosystems (IWSECO 2015)
  • Co-located with the European Conference on Software Architecture (ECSA 2015)
  • in Dubrovnik/Cavtat, Croatia
  • Organizers:
      • Jens Knodel, Fraunhofer Institute for Experimental Software Engineering (IESE), Germany
      • Mircea Lungu, University of Berne, Switzerland
      • Tom Mens, University of Mons, Belgium
  • Website: http://wea.github.io/history/wea2015/
  • Proceedings: tba.

WEA 2014

  • 2nd International Workshop on Ecosystem Architectures (WEA 2014)
  • Co-organized with the 6th Workshop on Software Ecosystems (IWSECO 2014)
  • Co-located with the European Conference on Software Architecture (ECSA 2014)
  • in Vienna, Austria
  • Organizers:
      • Peter Knauber, Mannheim, University of Applied Sciences, Germany
      • Jens Knodel, Fraunhofer Institute for Experimental Software Engineering (IESE), Germany
      • Mircea Lungu, University of Berne, Switzerland
  • Website: http://wea.github.io/history/wea2014/
  • Proceedings: http://dl.acm.org/citation.cfm?doid=2642803

WEA 2013

  • 1st International Workshop on Ecosystem Architectures (WEA 2013)
  • Co-located with the 9th Joint Meeting of European Software Engineering Conference and the ACM SIGSOFT Symposium on the Foundations of Software (ESEC/FSE)
  • in St. Petersburg, Russia
  • Organizers:
      • Peter Knauber, Mannheim, University of Applied Sciences, Germany
      • Jens Knodel, Fraunhofer Institute for Experimental Software Engineering (IESE), Germany
      • Mircea Lungu, University of Berne, Switzerland
  • Website: http://wea.github.io/history/wea2013/
  • Proceedings: http://dl.acm.org/citation.cfm?id=2501585
