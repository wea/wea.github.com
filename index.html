This is the tenative program. We might still change it a bit, but there is one main constraint: the workshop should give everybody the chance to present his work and interact with everybody else. Together we should define challenges and find solutions for developers, integrators, and architects who work in large open-source or industrial ecosystems.


Talks can be anywhere between 10 and 20 minutes and will be followed by discussions.
