WELCOME

WEA 2014 – 2nd International Workshop on Ecosystem Architectures

The 2nd International Workshop on Ecosystem Architectures (WEA 2014) will be co-organized with the 6th Workshop on Software Ecosystems (IWSECO 2014).
WEA 2014 and IWSECO 2014 will be co-located with the European Conference on Software Architecture (ECSA 2014) from August 25 – 29, 2014 at University of Vienna, Austria.

IMPORTANT DATES

June 06, 2014       -->  Submission 
June 30, 2014       -->  Notification
July 07, 2014       -->  Camera Ready
August 25–26, 2014  -->  IWSECO & WEA (Satellite Event of ECSA)
August 27–29, 2014  -->  ECSA Main Conference

HOW TO SUBMIT?

Submissions must follow the ACM SIG Proceedings Style (see templates).
Papers should not exceed 6 pages and be submitted in electronic form (PDF) using EasyChair:
https://www.easychair.org/conferences/?conf=iwsecowea2014
