WELCOME

We are looking forward to meeting you at WEA 2017: a Workshop on Software Ecosystem Architectures, where ideas and insights can be shared with peers from the same area of research. Benefit from interesting and fruitful discussions, exchange lessons learned, meet peers working in the area, and make new friends!

The 5th International Workshop on Ecosystem Architectures (WEA 2017) will be co-located with the joint meeting of the European Software Engineering Conference and the ACM SIGSOFT Symposium on the Foundations of Software Engineering (ESEC/FSE 2017).
